About Us

New Face Ghana Foundation (NFGF), a non-governmental organization formed by a team of young energetic and creative individuals who believe in change. NEW Face Ghana Foundation is incorporated to organize, undertake, sponsor and promote health, peace and to reduce unemployment by creation of opportunities for the youth towards betterment of Ghana.

Our Vision

NEW FACE GHANA FOUNDATION seeks to organize, undertake, sponsor, and promote health, peace and to reduce unemployment by creation of opportunities for the youth towards betterment of Ghana freed of politics.
